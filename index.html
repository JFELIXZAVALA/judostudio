Our Programs

  • Judo Kids

    Our young Judoka's program is for kids between the ages of 5 and 15. Children will be introduced to Judo essential techniques, focus on self-defense, in a safe, fun, and friendly Dojo. A variety of drills are used to teach the kids learn while having fun. Kids will learn about Judo values and principles, such as respect, discipline, and confidence, in order to improve mind, body, and technique. In addition, kids will be practicing together with other Judo Kids, developing social skills, teamwork, and focus.

    Discover More

  • Adult Judokas

    The Adult Judo program teaches self-defense skills and techniques with a focus on developing physical skills and well-being. The training atmosphere is safe, supportive and non-competitive, since Judo emphasizes the team work in order to achieve individual goals. Students will be learning on an individual level, but they will also be learning with their fellow class mates and instructors, which will naturally help create a space where everyone can learn together, as well as socialize together.
